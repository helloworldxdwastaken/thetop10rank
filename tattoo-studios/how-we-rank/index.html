Ranking Methodology

Our objective, data-driven approach to evaluating service providers.

Our 6-Point Evaluation Standard

We use a weighted scoring model to ensure fairness and accuracy.

  • Reputation (30%): Analysis of reviews across Google, Yelp, and industry-specific platforms.
  • Expertise (25%): Verification of licenses, certifications, and years in business.
  • Portfolio/Results (20%): Visual assessment of past work or verified client case studies.
  • Customer Service (10%): Response times, booking ease, and complaint resolution.
  • Facilities (10%): Hygiene, equipment quality, and atmosphere.
  • Value (5%): Pricing transparency vs. market average.
