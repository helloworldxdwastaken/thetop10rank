Banking in France: What You Need to Know

Opening a bank account in France is essential for anyone living or working in the country. Note that traditional banks often require an appointment to open an account, while online banks (like BoursoBank) offer a faster, fully digital process.

Types of French Banks

  • Traditional Banks (Banques de Réseau): Large networks with physical branches (BNP, Société Générale). Good for complex needs.
  • Mutual/Cooperative Banks: Owned by customers (Crédit Agricole, Banque Populaire). Often have strong regional roots.
  • Online Banks (Banques en Ligne): Often subsidiaries of big banks (BoursoBank is owned by Société Générale). Offer lower fees but no physical branches.
