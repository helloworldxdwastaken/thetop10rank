What Makes a Great Bank?

Choosing a bank is a critical financial decision. Our ranking methodology considers the factors that impact your daily life and long-term security:

Financial Stability

30% weight

Capital adequacy, history, and resilience in economic downturns.

Digital Experience

25% weight

App usability, feature set, and online banking reliability.

Customer Service

20% weight

Support availability, resolution speed, and branch network.

Fees & Rates

15% weight

Transparency of fees and competitiveness of interest rates.

Account Features

10% weight

Perks, rewards programs, and flexibility of account options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you verify bank safety?

We prioritize banks that are insured by their respective national deposit insurance schemes (e.g., FDIC in the US, FGD in France). We also review credit ratings from major agencies like Moody's and S&P.

Are online-only banks safe?

Yes, legitimate online banks (neobanks) are typically regulated just like traditional banks. However, we always check their banking license status and partner banks before ranking them.

Do you cover business banking?

Currently, our primary rankings focus on personal banking. We plan to add specific business banking rankings in late 2026.
