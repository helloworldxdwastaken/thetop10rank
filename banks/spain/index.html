Banking in Spain: Key Considerations

Spain's banking sector has consolidated significantly. The "Big Three" (CaixaBank, Santander, BBVA) dominate the market. Many banks charge maintenance commissions if demanding conditions (direct debit of salary, etc.) are not met, so reading the fine print is crucial.

NIE Requirement

To open a resident bank account, you typically need a NIE (Foreigner Identity Number). Non-resident accounts are available but often come with higher fees.
