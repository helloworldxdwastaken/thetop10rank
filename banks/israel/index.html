Banking in Israel: Key Insights

The Israeli banking system is advanced but can be bureaucratic. Fees (Amalot) are a common complaint, so negotiating account terms or joining specific "clubs" (Soldiers, Students, Engineers) can save significant money.

Oleh Hadash Benefits

New immigrants (Olim) are entitled to special banking benefits, including fee waivers and favorable loan terms during their first years. All major banks have dedicated English-speaking desks for Olim.

Digital Revolution

Digital wallets like Bit (Hapoalim generally) and PayBox (Discount) are ubiquitous for P2P payments in Israel, making bank choice slightly less critical for daily transfers.
