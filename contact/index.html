Response Times

  • General inquiries: 3 business days
  • Business suggestions: 10 business days (eligibility review)
  • Corrections: 5 business days (investigation)
  • Press inquiries: 24-48 hours

Response times may be longer during peak periods (January, ranking publication weeks). We'll acknowledge receipt within 24 hours.

What We Can't Help With

  • Ranking disputes without factual errors: Businesses cannot appeal subjective scores. We only reconsider if factual mistakes affected evaluation.
  • Requests to exclude competitors: We don't accept requests to remove or downrank specific businesses.
  • Individual service recommendations: We publish rankings but don't provide personal consulting on which business to choose.
  • Paid placement requests: Rankings cannot be purchased. Don't ask—it undermines our credibility.
