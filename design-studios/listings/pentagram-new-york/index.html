Why Pentagram New York Ranks #1

Pentagram's New York office represents the gold standard in design consultancy, combining 50+ years of brand expertise with an unmatched portfolio spanning corporate identity, cultural institutions, and editorial design. Their partner-led model ensures senior designers remain deeply involved throughout projects, and their process balances rigorous strategic thinking with exceptional craft.

What separates Pentagram from competitors is consistency across decades. While many studios shine for 3-5 year periods, Pentagram has sustained excellence since the 1970s, continuously evolving while maintaining core principles of intelligent, purposeful design. Their work for Mastercard, MIT Media Lab, and The Public Theater showcases versatility without compromising distinctive quality.

Services & Specializations

Brand Identity

Comprehensive systems including strategy, naming, logo design, typography, color, and application guidelines. Particular strength in complex multi-brand architectures.

Editorial Design

Publication design, art direction, and content systems. Deep expertise in magazine redesigns, book design, and editorial brand systems.

Environmental Graphics

Wayfinding, signage, and spatial branding for physical spaces. Strong track record with museums, corporate headquarters, and public spaces.

Digital Design

Website design and digital brand applications. Less focus on complex product UX/UI compared to digital-native studios.

Notable Projects & Portfolio Highlights

Mastercard Rebrand (2016)

Pentagram's redesign of Mastercard's identity simplified the iconic overlapping circles while creating a flexible system that works across digital and physical touchpoints. The project demonstrated their ability to evolve heritage brands without losing equity, introducing the "sonic brand" concept and creating a symbol-only logo variant years before competitors.

MIT Media Lab Identity (2011)

A revolutionary generative identity system that creates unique variations while maintaining coherent brand recognition. The project influenced an entire generation of dynamic brand systems and showcased Pentagram's willingness to embrace experimental approaches for the right clients.

The Public Theater (Updated 2020s)

Ongoing evolution of the bold typographic system originally designed by Paula Scher. This long-term partnership demonstrates Pentagram's ability to maintain brand consistency while keeping visual language fresh across decades.

Who Should Hire Pentagram New York

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get in touch with Pentagram New York?

Initial inquiries go through Pentagram's website contact form. Be prepared to provide project overview, budget range, and timeline. The studio typically responds within 3-5 business days with next steps or availability.

Can Pentagram work within smaller budgets?

Pentagram's structure and operational costs dictate 6-figure minimums for most projects. However, they occasionally offer "brand sprint" engagements ($50k-75k) for organizations they find mission-aligned, particularly nonprofits and cultural institutions.

How does Pentagram's partnership model work?

Pentagram operates as a partnership of independent designers. Each partner runs their own projects and team. This means you're effectively hiring a specific partner's practice within the Pentagram framework. Always clarify which partner will lead your project.

Does Pentagram do implementation or just design?

Pentagram designs systems and guidelines but typically doesn't handle ongoing production or website development. They partner with implementation studios or hand off detailed specifications to client teams/vendors.
